Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about getting started with QuickTask

All gigs are pre-screened by our team and university partners. We verify every employer and only approve tasks that meet our safety standards for students.

Earnings vary by task type and time commitment. Most students earn £50-300 per month working 5-10 hours weekly. Popular tasks include tutoring (£15-25/hour), event assistance (£12-18/hour), and research help (£10-15/hour).

We offer tutoring, research assistance, event support, content creation, data entry, campus tours, and more. All tasks are designed to fit around your academic schedule.

No prior experience required! Many tasks are entry-level and perfect for building your first professional experiences while studying.

Payments are processed securely through our platform within 24-48 hours of task completion. You can track all earnings in your dashboard.
